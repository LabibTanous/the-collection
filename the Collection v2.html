The number

Why ninety-nine.

Not ninety-eight. Not a hundred. Ninety-nine — a number small enough to mean something, large enough to reach the right people.

Every piece is numbered by hand. When the last one is gone, the run is retired. No restocks. No seconds. No exceptions. The number you claim is yours permanently, recorded and sent with your piece on a signed edition card.

The first to claim get the lowest numbers.
